The TEAM

Chief Executive Officer

After years on hospital wards, I witnessed firsthand how small communication gaps could impact patient wellbeing and staff efficiency. TruSelv was born from that experience — to bring empathy, clarity, and technology together at the bedside. Our mission is simple: to help every patient feel seen, informed, and cared for — beyond the diagnosis..

Eric Tweneboah / Founder

Chief Operations Officer

I am a daughter, mother, grandmother and former foster parent who has spent over 40 years in the NHS as a nurse, educator and leader. I’ve worked across multiple specialities and supported people and their loved ones from the very beginning of life to the end of it. My drive has always been to provide compassionate, person-centred and inclusive care, rooted in dignity and respect. I believe in lifelong learning, staying curious and embracing change. Innovation isn’t just about new ideas; it’s about having the bravery and willingness to evolve.

Michaela Reynolds / Co-founder (COO)
