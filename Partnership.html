How Health Facilities Can Work With Truselv to Transform Patient Experience

At Truselv, we believe the future of healthcare is built on collaboration, innovation, and compassion. Our ecosystem — BEDBORD, FOLDA, NGAGE, and TEEVY — redefines how hospitals and care homes connect with patients, manage information, and deliver personalized care. But none of this happens in isolation. It begins with partnership.

This blog explains how healthcare providers across the UK and beyond can partner with Truselv — and what the journey looks like, from first contact to full implementation.

Transforming care environments through collaboration, technology, and shared purpose.

Step 1: Initial Contact — Understanding Your Vision

The partnership begins with a conversation. Whether you’re a hospital administrator, care home director, or NHS Trust manager, our team will arrange a free consultation to understand your facility’s current setup, challenges, and aspirations.

  • Discovery Call: A 30–60 minute conversation to discuss your facility’s needs and care priorities.
  • Assessment: Review of your existing infrastructure — from bedside environments to digital record systems.
  • Goal Alignment: Identify how our products can enhance patient care, staff efficiency, and engagement.
“Every great partnership starts with listening — understanding what truly matters to your patients and your people.”

Step 2: Tailored Proposal & Solution Design

Once we understand your needs, our team designs a bespoke proposal outlining how our technologies fit your environment. We’ll show you exactly how BEDBORD can streamline patient data, how FOLDA can digitize records, how NGAGE can support cognitive health, and how TEEVY can improve family connection and entertainment.

  • Custom Configuration: Solutions tailored to your patient demographics and care setting.
  • Integration Plan: Mapping with your existing IT systems and care workflows.
  • Investment Overview: Transparent pricing, cost-benefit analysis, and funding guidance.

Each solution is designed collaboratively — ensuring a perfect fit for your care environment.

Step 3: Pilot Installation & Staff Training

We believe in proving value early. Most facilities start with a pilot program — typically one ward, unit, or care home floor — to evaluate the impact of our solutions in real conditions. During this phase, Truselv provides full technical setup and on-site staff training.

  • Pilot Setup: Installation of selected devices and systems, configured to your requirements.
  • Staff Workshops: Hands-on training for nurses, administrators, and activity coordinators.
  • Feedback Loop: Continuous support to measure satisfaction, efficiency, and engagement improvements.
“We don’t just install technology — we empower your team to use it confidently, effectively, and compassionately.”

Step 4: Full Implementation — Bringing Digital Care to Life

After a successful pilot, we move into full rollout. Our project management team oversees every stage — from hardware delivery and installation to digital integration and go-live support. Every deployment is conducted with minimal disruption to daily operations.

  • System Integration: Seamless connectivity across devices and platforms.
  • Testing & Validation: Robust checks to ensure data security, compliance, and reliability.
  • Onboarding Support: Dedicated rollout specialists to ensure a smooth transition.

Our implementation teams handle every detail — ensuring a safe, secure, and seamless deployment.

Step 5: Ongoing Support, Analytics & Growth

Our partnership doesn’t end at installation. Truselv provides continuous system monitoring, data analytics, and regular software updates to keep your environment running at its best. Quarterly reviews ensure the system continues to deliver measurable results and evolve with your needs.

  • 24/7 Support: Technical and clinical helpdesk assistance for your staff.
  • Performance Analytics: Monthly insights on usage, engagement, and outcomes.
  • Innovation Reviews: Access to new features, upgrades, and co-development opportunities.
“Partnership means progress. We grow together, continuously refining our solutions to meet your evolving goals.”

Typical Partnership Timeline

Phase Activity Estimated Duration
