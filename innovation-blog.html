Innovation in Care Environments: How Digital Healthcare Solutions Are Transforming Patient Care

Healthcare is evolving—and fast. Hospitals and care homes are under immense pressure to deliver high-quality care while improving efficiency, patient engagement, and overall wellbeing. At the heart of this transformation is digital healthcare innovation. Our suite of flagship solutions—BEDBORD, FOLDA, NGAGE, and TEEVY—represents the future of patient engagement technology and hospital innovation.

BEDBORD: Redefining the Patient Bedspace

BEDBORD: 20-inch interactive bedside display for patient information and care schedules

Traditional bed boards are static and outdated. BEDBORD replaces them with a dynamic, connected solution. It displays patient information, treatment schedules, and care plans in real-time—accessible to both staff and patients.

  • Streamlines clinical workflows with instant access to patient data
  • Reduces administrative errors through centralized digital records
  • Enhances patient experience with clear, understandable information

BEDBORD is more than a display—it’s the foundation for a digitally connected patient environment.

FOLDA: Smarter Patient Notes, Better Care

FOLDA: Next-generation patient note app for real-time documentation

Accurate and timely documentation is essential for safe, effective care. FOLDA allows healthcare professionals to record, store, and access patient notes instantly across devices. Fully compliant with GDPR and NHS data regulations, FOLDA ensures information is secure while improving operational efficiency.

  • Real-time synchronization across tablets, phones, and bedside displays
  • Analytics and reporting tools to monitor patient progress
  • Simplified workflow reduces staff workload and improves accuracy

FOLDA empowers healthcare teams to deliver safer, faster, and more informed care.

NGAGE: Interactive Digital Activities for Patients

NGAGE: Interactive games and activities to stimulate patient minds and prevent boredom

Boredom and cognitive decline are common challenges for patients, especially those with dementia. NGAGE provides a suite of games and digital activities designed to stimulate the mind and keep patients active.

  • Offers dementia-friendly games and cognitive exercises
  • Encourages mental stimulation and social interaction
  • Reduces feelings of isolation and boredom during hospital or care home stays
  • Continuously updated with new apps and activities for patient engagement

NGAGE makes staying mentally active fun, interactive, and meaningful—a game-changing solution for patient wellbeing.

TEEVY: The Ultimate Bedside Entertainment & Communication Hub

TEEVY: Android-powered bedside TV with access to all channels, games, and direct video calls to family

TEEVY is an Android-powered wall-mounted TV that goes far beyond traditional hospital TVs. Patients can access all TV and radio channels, play games, and even video call family members directly—without needing to go through a nurse’s phone. Family members are given a dedicated number upon admission for instant contact.

  • Comprehensive entertainment: TV, radio, and interactive games
  • Facilitates meaningful family connections through direct video calls
  • Enhances patient comfort and reduces feelings of isolation
  • Touch-enabled, intuitive interface for all patients

TEEVY combines entertainment, connection, and care—making hospital stays more comfortable and engaging.

Why Digital Healthcare Solutions Are the Future

Together, BEDBORD, FOLDA, NGAGE, and TEEVY form a fully integrated ecosystem designed to modernize hospitals and care homes. By combining patient engagement technology with operational efficiency and secure data management, healthcare providers can:

  • Improve patient outcomes and satisfaction
  • Reduce administrative errors and staff burden
  • Keep patients mentally active, engaged, and connected to loved ones

The future of healthcare is digital, interactive, and patient-centered. Investing in innovation today ensures hospitals and care homes are ready for tomorrow.

Digital healthcare solutions aren’t just tools—they are the new standard for modern care.
