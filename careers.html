How we hire

We hire for mission alignment, operational excellence, and real-world empathy. Interviews include a short technical / portfolio review (where relevant), a culture-fit conversation with the founding team, and a final discussion with a clinical advisor for patient-facing roles.

What we offer
  • Competitive salary & early-stage equity options (where applicable)
  • Flexible / hybrid working and generous leave
  • Professional development budget & healthcare allowance
  • Opportunity to shape a product used in hospitals and care homes
Equal opportunity
