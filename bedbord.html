BEDBORD: Redefining the Patient Bedspace with Smart, Connected Care

Hospitals and care homes have long relied on traditional whiteboards to communicate patient information — names, care notes, and schedules hastily written with markers that fade or smudge. While simple, these boards represent a missed opportunity for innovation in healthcare communication. Enter BEDBORD — a revolutionary 20-inch digital display designed to transform the patient environment, streamline clinical workflows, and humanize the care experience.

BEDBORD: The next-generation digital bedside board for hospitals and care homes.

A Modern Replacement for the Traditional Bed Board

BEDBORD replaces the outdated handwritten boards found above hospital beds with a sleek, wall-mounted digital display. Fully customizable and connected to hospital systems, it presents accurate and up-to-date patient information securely and efficiently.

Rather than relying on manual updates, staff can automatically sync patient details — such as name, allergies, assigned nurse, meal preferences, or care plan — directly from hospital records. The result? Fewer errors, faster updates, and a clearer, more professional environment for both patients and visitors.

“BEDBORD bridges the gap between clinical accuracy and human connection — transforming every bedspace into an intelligent, patient-centered care station.”

Designed for the Realities of Modern Healthcare

Healthcare environments are busy, complex, and high-pressure. BEDBORD was designed hand-in-hand with clinicians, nurses, and patient experience teams to fit seamlessly into existing routines while removing unnecessary burdens. Its intuitive touchscreen interface and modular software make it easy to use — whether displaying vital patient info or integrating new communication tools.

  • Automatic Sync: Instantly updates patient data from the hospital’s electronic health record (EHR) system.
  • Customizable Display: Each department can tailor what’s shown — from clinical notes to welcome messages.
  • Touch & Voice Enabled: Simple controls allow nurses or patients to adjust views and settings effortlessly.
  • Secure by Design: Fully compliant with NHS and GDPR data standards.

From care homes to intensive care wards, BEDBORD’s adaptability makes it a game-changer for any care setting. It’s more than technology — it’s an enabler of safer, more transparent, and more compassionate care.

Improving Communication and Reducing Errors

One of the biggest challenges in healthcare is miscommunication — a leading cause of errors and inefficiencies. BEDBORD tackles this issue head-on by ensuring critical patient details are always clear, current, and visible to everyone who needs them.

Its integration with hospital data systems ensures updates happen automatically — from patient transfers to staff handovers. No more confusion, no more illegible handwriting, no more missed details.

BEDBORD’s intuitive interface ensures real-time, error-free communication across the care team.

Empowering Patients and Families

BEDBORD doesn’t just serve staff — it empowers patients too. The display can be configured to show helpful, non-clinical information like visiting hours, daily meal options, or activity schedules. In care homes, it can even display personalized welcome messages, birthdays, or reminders, helping residents feel seen and valued.

By promoting transparency and clarity, BEDBORD helps reduce anxiety for patients and relatives, creating a sense of trust and reassurance in every interaction.

Sustainability and Efficiency Built In

Replacing disposable paper signs and whiteboard markers with a digital platform dramatically cuts waste and long-term costs. BEDBORD’s energy-efficient display consumes minimal power and requires almost no maintenance, making it an eco-friendly upgrade that delivers measurable savings over time.

  • Reduced administrative workload for nursing staff
  • Lower operational costs through automation
  • Eco-friendly alternative to paper-based processes

A Step Toward the Future of Care

BEDBORD represents more than innovation — it represents a cultural shift toward dignity, clarity, and digital empowerment in healthcare. By giving staff better tools and patients a clearer voice, we’re building the foundations of a more connected, compassionate, and efficient care environment.

“When every bedspace becomes intelligent, every patient benefits. That’s the promise of BEDBORD.”
